Fun things to do in Siem Reap

There are so many to choose from that you’ll never have to worry about what to do in Siem Reap. Whether you’re seeking a bird’s eye view of the region, are willing to get down and dirty on dusty countryside rides, or are ready to be amazed by daring acrobatic feats woven into engaging storylines, we’ve got you covered.

Apsara Dinner Show

$25

Enjoy a spectacular Apsara performance, a Khmer dance that’s inspired by the water and cloud spirits of Hinduism and Buddhism. Treat your palate to an array of flavors from both the East and the West while watching ancient Khmer tales come to life.

Quad Bike Adventure

$75

Siem Reap Quad Bike Adventures brings you to the heart of “The Real Cambodia” where you will be happily welcomed into the local schools, traditional homes, active Buddhist monasteries, rice paddy fields, lotus farms and remote Pre-Angkorean temples with unmatched sunset views. There’s so much to see on the Quad Bike Adventure, we highly recommend checking out these “off the beaten path” activities and beautiful sights during your visit to Siem Reap.

Khmer Cooking Class

$25

Cook delicious Khmer food taught by a Cambodian Chef. We offer classes tailored by enthusiast chef Mr. Sophose and the preparation of very popular Cambodian dishes made from fresh local ingredients in our regular daily class. Enjoy cooking class at the countryside with a green garden. Authentic experience in a Khmer family house. All classes are hands-on and ingredients are provided. You can enjoy learning whatever your level of cooking is.
